Who We Are

Welcome to Fortune 401(k), Inc.

Fortune 401(k), Inc. is an Atlanta based Third Party Administrator dedicated to helping Americans build secure futures through expertly designed qualified retirement plans. Founded in 1992, we specialize in self-directed 401(k) plans and serve businesses of every size—from solo entrepreneurs to large corporations.

Our mission is simple: remove the complexity of retirement plan compliance, design, and management so business owners and plan sponsors can focus on growth. We deliver full-service administration, IRS and DOL compliance, annual testing, valuations, enrollment support, payroll integration, and personalized consulting—all with fast response times and a relationship-first approach.

What We Offer

Qualified Retirement Plans

We work with sole proprietors all the way up to large corporations — designing and administering the right plan for your unique situation.

401(k) Plans

Flexible and popular — featuring employee salary deferrals, employer matching contributions, profit sharing, and self-directed investment options.

Profit Sharing Plans

Employer-funded plans with discretionary contributions — ideal for companies with variable annual profits who want to reward employees.

Defined Benefit Plans

Guaranteed retirement benefit plans — perfect for business owners seeking to maximize annual tax-deductible contributions beyond 401(k) limits.

Cash Balance Plans

A powerful hybrid combining defined benefit security with defined contribution flexibility — excellent for high-income professionals.

ESOPs

Employee Stock Ownership Plans for succession planning & tax-advantaged employee ownership — a unique benefit for business transitions.

Custom Solutions

Money Purchase Plans, Target Benefit Plans, and fully custom combinations tailored to your specific workforce and retirement goals.
